Visit Mangalore

Discover the timeless charm of Mangalore, where coastal serenity embraces rich heritage, creating a symphony of experiences that resonate with every wanderer's soul.

These activities will immerse you in the essence of Mangalore

Beach Bliss

Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of Mangalore's pristine beaches like Panambur Beach and Tannirbhavi Beach. Enjoy water sports, take a leisurely stroll, or simply unwind while the waves serenade your senses.

Temple Trails

Embark on a spiritual journey by exploring the intricately designed temples of Mangalore, such as the Kudroli Gokarnath Temple and Kadri Manjunath Temple, where ancient architecture and devotion converge.

Culinary Odyssey

Indulge your taste buds in a gastronomic adventure through Mangalore's diverse cuisine. Relish the iconic Mangalorean fish curry, savor local delicacies like Neer Dosa, and delight in the region's unique blend of flavors.

St. Aloysius Charm

Witness artistry come to life at St. Aloysius Chapel, adorned with stunning frescoes that narrate stories from the Bible. The exquisite paintings and architecture make it a true visual masterpiece.

Udupi Exploration

Venture to Udupi, a nearby town renowned for its ancient Krishna Temple and delectable Udupi cuisine. The spiritual atmosphere and traditional dishes offer an authentic cultural experience.

Pilikula Nisargadhama

Engage with nature at Pilikula Nisargadhama, a sprawling eco-education and tourism development project. Explore the zoo, botanical garden, science center, and a serene lake, making it an ideal family outing.
